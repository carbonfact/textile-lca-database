# Documentation

> Part of [Circular Knitting Methodology](README.md)

## 1. Background

### 1.1 Context

This document summarises the modelling approach for **circular knitting**, representing a typical industrial process for jersey fabric production.

The purpose is to make the process transparent and reproducible across studies, while allowing refinement as factory-specific data become available.

### 1.2 Scope and functional unit

- **Functional unit:** 1 kg of knitted fabric.
- **Technology:** single and double jersey circular knitting.
- **Included:** yarn losses/waste, lubricants, energy consumption, electricity, indirect energy allocation, capital goods and their end-of-life (EoL).
- **Excluded:** fibre and yarn manufacturing (modelled separately), building construction, packaging and logistics.
