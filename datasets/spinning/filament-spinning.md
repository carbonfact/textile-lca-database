# Filament Spinning

> Part of [Spinning](README.md)

**15 datasets** | Functional unit: 1 kg filament yarn

Filament spinning covers the extrusion of polymer melts or solutions into continuous filaments. Technologies include melt spinning (POY and FDY/DTY), wet spinning (for MMCF like viscose and lyocell), and dry spinning (for elastane and specialty fibres). Filament processes are generally less energy-intensive per kg than staple yarn spinning.
