# Natural Rubber (STR Production)

> Lifecycle assessment datasets for Standard Technical Rubber (STR) production, covering cradle-to-gate impacts with country-specific land use change (LUC) and peatland drainage emissions.

**4 datasets** | Functional unit: 1 kg STR | Methodology covers all 16 EF 3.1 impact indicators

## Overview

This process category covers natural rubber production — specifically the production of Standard Technical Rubber (STR), a natural rubber derivative used for outsole manufacturing in the footwear industry. The system boundary is cradle-to-gate, from rubber plantation (field coagulum production with economic allocation) through STR processing.

Four country-specific datasets are provided for the major rubber-producing countries: Thailand (TH), Indonesia (ID), Côte d'Ivoire (CI), and Vietnam (VN). Each variant captures country-specific land use change (LUC) emissions, peatland drainage emissions, electricity grid mix, and crop yield.
