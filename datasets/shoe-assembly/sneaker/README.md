# Sneaker Shoe Assembly

> Lifecycle assessment datasets for sneaker footwear assembly operations, covering chemicals, energy, water, packaging, emissions and waste across 9 sub-processes.

**2 datasets** | Functional unit: 1 pair of footwear | Methodology covers all 16 EF 3.1 impact indicators

## Overview

This process category covers sneaker shoe assembly — the process-level operations involved in assembling footwear, excluding physical materials and components. The system boundary includes lamination, cutting, HF welding, screen printing, insole, nosew, stitching, stock fit, and final assembly. Two electricity scenarios are provided: Vietnam (VN, primary data origin) and Global average (GLO).

The database was developed based on primary data from a supplier in Vietnam using a modular approach by process and chemical families.
